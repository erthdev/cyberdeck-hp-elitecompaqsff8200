## April 28, 2026 (PSU Incident)

Today I tried to open and modify the original PSU for the build. After reconnecting it to the system, it immediately failed and triggered 4 blinking lights. The PSU is now completely unusable.

This confirmed that modifying the original power supply is not safe or viable for this project. The failure happened instantly, showing how risky PSU experimentation can be.

From here, the build is fully switching to a Pico PSU setup.

## What I learned

PSU modification is high-risk and not worth attempting in this build
Failure can happen instantly and permanently
Safer modular power solutions are the better direction
Current status

Original PSU is dead. Build is now committed to Pico PSU power.
